About Me

I was born on December 12, 1996 in Lagos, Nigeria. Lagos also being the place I was raised is where I have spent most of my years. I came to Canada in 2014 where I settled into the quiet city of Hamilton before moving into my current location in Mississauga. I love to travel, eat, play and make video-games, swim, code, and so on. I cannot dance to save my life and I hate summer but love the winter. I love watching horror movies, thriller movies, and movies that leave you googling the end of the movie. Christopher Nolan is my favourite movie director and Hideo Kojima is my favourive game designer. I spend most of my days doing course work but when i'm not breaking my back writing a 4000 word essay I am usually with friends eating out at a restaurant. Chinese buffet is a must when I go out. After graduating I plan on doing my masters in either web development, video game development or software programming.
